Santa

Santa’s Enchanted Forest has been a South Florida annual holiday tradition for more than 37 years. Each year, hundreds of thousands of Miami Dade residents visit Santa’s, including more than 30,000 underprivileged children and families that are admitted FREE. As SEF’s lease with Miami-Dade County comes to an end in March 2020 and the land at Tropical Park undergoes the lengthy duration of a new solicitation process for 2021 and beyond, the community is at risk of losing this beloved holiday event and over 1000 jobs for the 2020 holiday season. While Santa's plans on bidding for a new long term lease agreement and spending millions of dollars in park improvements, that decision will not take place in time for the 2020 season.
